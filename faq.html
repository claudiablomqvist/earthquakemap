Ritcherskalan

The Richter scale is used to measure how strong an earthquake is. The scale is presented from 1 to 9, a higher magnitude means greater damage to the surroundings. Below and on the youtube clip it's described what the magnitude of the Richter scale means.

  1. Can't feel
  2. Hard to feel the earthquake. No damage to any house
  3. Starts shaking and can be felt
  4. The earthquake is noticeable
  5. Damage to poorly constructed houses can occur
  6. Serious damage to houses can occur
  7. Many houses can collapse
  8. Can cause very serious injuries, most houses collapse
  9. Everything breaks down
